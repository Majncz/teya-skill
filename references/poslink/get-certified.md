# Stage 4: Get Certified

The Certification Process marks the conclusion of the integration process and it's the last step before going live.

## Prerequisites

> **Before scheduling your Certification, please make sure that:**
> - Your integration passed the previous tests successfully
> - Checks all items in the Integration Check List
> - Send to partnersupport@teya.com the diagram showing how the ePOS deals with Authentication and Refresh of Tokens in the background

## Certification

During this stage, your ePOS integration will be tested alongside with a team from Teya. During the Certification it will be requested for you to share the ePOS screen and perform live the following test cases:

1. **[Mandatory]** Setup Flow Test Cases
2. **[Mandatory]** Payment Flow Test Cases
3. **[Mandatory]** Cancellation Flow Test Cases
4. **[Optional]** Refund Flow Test Cases
5. **[Optional]** Tips Flow Test Cases

Please feel free to contact our team at partnersupport@teya.com to schedule your Certification. Once all tests are successfully completed, your ePOS is officially ready to begin piloting with real merchants.

> **Important:** 
> - All Certifications are valid for the period of one year.
> - In case of changes or implementation of new Flows on your ePOS, a new Certification is required.

If you have any questions or need further assistance please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team by sending an email to partnersupport@teya.com.
