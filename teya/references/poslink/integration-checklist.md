# Before you Start

> **Important:** These steps are essential for successful integration. Please complete all steps before proceeding to development.

## Step 1: Get your Developer Access Account

**Access the development environment**

- Go to **[partner.teya.xyz](https://partner.teya.xyz)** to access the development environment
- If you don't have access, contact your Partnership Manager or [partnersupport@teya.com](mailto:partnersupport@teya.com)

## Step 2: Request your Testing Toolkit

**Contact your Partnership Manager to receive:**

- **Test Account** - A complete test merchant account for development
- **Payment Application** - APK with pre-built test cards for various scenarios
- **Physical Card Machine** (Optional) - The payment application can run on any Android emulator

> **Important:** Development happens in the development environment. Once completed and certified, you'll receive production credentials for live transactions. The development environment is a real environment that can simulate the same scenarios as production.
